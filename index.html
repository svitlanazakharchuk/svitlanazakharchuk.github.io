DEEP UNDERSTANDING
OF MEDICAL MALPRACTICE

Reviewing medical records in medical malpractice cases is one of the most crucial tasks towards understanding what happened in a case. However, the current process is extremely time-consuming, expensive, tedious, and error-prone. Most medical records lack clarity and are voluminous, disorganized, out of chronological order, and poorly scanned. These obstacles make it extraordinarily costly to locate the relevant sections, resulting in inefficiency, wasted attorney time, and high litigation costs.

OUR SOLUTION

Intrellit produces chronological narrative reports of medical records using Artificial Intelligence (AI) and does so at a fraction of the cost currently paid by law firms that delegate this task to associates, paralegals, and outsourcing companies. Our reports cover the entire chronology of a case, pulling information from various sets of medical records, and hone in on only the most relevant issues. The superior quality of our reports stems from our use of cutting edge technology and our years of experience in medical malpractice litigation. Our New York admitted attorneys, with the assistance of AI, are able to draft high-quality reports in a fraction of the time. To accomplish this, we use proprietary software that (1) digitalizes poorly scanned records 3 times more accurately than digitization technology currently available to law firms and (2) utilizes a smart-search tool, powered by AI, which reduces many time-consuming, tedious, and error-prone steps.
